What We're Building
01
Frontend Vertical

Future
Fuels

Converting bamboo and agricultural residues into next-generation coal alternatives and advanced carbon products for industry and export.

Pellets & Briquettes — High-GCV coal substitutes for thermal power and industrial boilers
Biochar & Black Pellets — Advanced carbon products for steel PCI and premium energy markets
Activated Carbon — For fertilisers, water filtration, specialty chemicals and industrial applications
Feedstock — Bamboo-primary, with rice straw, cotton stalk and mixed agri-residue
02
Backend Vertical

Ecosystem
Development

Building the ground infrastructure that makes sustainable biomass supply chains viable — from farmer to factory gate.

Sustainable Harvest — Responsible bamboo harvesting protocols with community co-management
Farmer Empowerment — Tools, equipment and training for harvest, grading and quality management
Quality & Grading — Age grading, species recognition and GI-mapped bamboo for fair trade premiums
Commercial Plantation — Bamboo plantation and farming development across target geographies
